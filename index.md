## Terminology
* **OOC (Out of Character)**: Communication or actions made by you, the player—not your character. Used for coordination or clarification without affecting RP.

* **IC (In Character)**: All actions, dialogue, and behavior performed by your character within the roleplay world. IC behavior should be consistent with your character’s traits and knowledge.

* **RP (Roleplay)**: The act of embodying a fictional character and participating in a shared narrative world with others.

* **Town Exclave**: A claimed area disconnected from the town’s main territory that is not an outpost.

* **Spaghetti Claims**: Claims that use narrow or single-chunk paths to stretch between larger land masses or gain extra distance.

## 1.0 General Rules
These rules apply across both the Minecraft server and the Alathra Beyond Discord. They represent the **baseline standard of behavior** expected from every member of the community. Mutual respect and personal responsibility are key to maintaining a safe and welcoming environment.

### 1.1 Respect staff decisions and follow moderator instructions
Do not argue with staff in public chats. If you have concerns or wish to appeal a decision, please use the [ticket system](https://discord.com/channels/1479820893957001216/1479864963307798640). The moderation team reserves the right to make judgment calls in the interest of the community. Respect their decisions even if you disagree.

### 1.2 Do not impersonate staff or act on their behalf
Pretending to be a staff member or enforcing rules yourself is not permitted.

### 1.3 Inappropriate or sexually explicit content is not allowed.
This includes suggestive language, imagery, or jokes. Keep all public content clean and appropriate for a diverse player base. Please avoid jokes about body parts, intimacy, or sexual innuendo or puns or phrases that could easily be misread as inappropriate by a younger audience.

### 1.4 Treat others with respect.
Discrimination, hate speech, and toxic behavior related to race, gender, beliefs, sexual orientation, or other personal attributes will not be tolerated.

### 1.5 Only report off-server incidents if they are serious.
Reports involving threats, slurs sent via DM to avoid rules, or explicit harassment are taken seriously. Otherwise, events that happen outside of Alathra Beyond should stay outside.

### 1.6 Use the correct chat channels.
Follow channel guidelines on both the server and Discord. Misusing channels disrupts communication and may result in punishment.

### 1.7 Advertising unrelated content without permission is not allowed.
Promoting non-Alathra Beyond content or servers is prohibited. If you're unsure whether something is allowed, open a [support ticket](https://discord.com/channels/1479820893957001216/1479864963307798640) to ask first.

### 1.8 Keep personal drama out of public spaces.
Personal conflicts should be handled in DMs. Repeated drama or conflicts that require staff involvement may result in punishment.

### 1.9 Harassment is not allowed.
Targeted slander, excessive lying, or hate directed at individuals, towns, or nations will be treated as harassment and dealt with accordingly.

### 1.10 Discussion of real-world politics or real-world religions is not allowed.
These topics can quickly become divisive. Please keep them out of public channels.

### 1.11 Alathra Beyond is not a mental health service.
While we care about our community, we are not equipped to provide support for mental health issues. If you are struggling, please seek help from a professional.
<br><sub>*Click here for [International Mental Health Hotlines](https://blog.opencounseling.com/suicide-hotlines/).*</sub>

### 1.12 Do not ping staff or staff roles in discord
We are a volunteer team and work hard to address every issue brought to us as quickly as possible. However this means that staff members may not be available when you create a ticket. We check every single ticket as soon as we are able but pinging staff in a ticket or in any discord channel is a punishable offense.

Exceptions:

* Bot Raids or other massively irregular and disruptive situations occurring on the server or discord.
* [NSFW](https://www.collinsdictionary.com/dictionary/english/nsfw) or [NSFL](https://www.collinsdictionary.com/dictionary/english/nsfl) content posted on the server or discord.
* Reasonable staff pings when you're engaging in a conversation with a staff member.

### 1.13 Do not DM staff members unprompted
For clarity and transparency we use a ticket system that can be found in the Alathra discord. Any and all issues, problems, or concerns involving Alathra that require staff attention should go through the ticket system.

### 1.14 Do not disrupt the server
Staff reserves the right to remove anyone from the community for generally poor behavior or disruptive acts that cause the community harm. Additionally, Staff reserves the right to remove anyone from the community for taking up too much staff resources.

## 2.0 Gameplay Rules
These rules define what is and isn’t permitted on the server to ensure a fair, and enjoyable experience for all players. This section covers policies on farming, automation, griefing, stealing, exploits, and building standards.

### 2.1 Fully automatic must farms be togglable.
Any farm that is fully automatic needs to be togglable and toggled off when you are not online. AFKing automatic farms is not allowed.

### 2.2 Griefing is not allowed in the Overworld.
This includes damaging your own town upon departure (self-griefing) or destroying ruins without rebuilding intent. This also includes naturally generated landscape.
<br><sub>*Renovation or constructive reuse of ruins is allowed.*</sub>

### 2.3 Stealing is allowed.
Theft is permitted only if it avoids illegal methods such as:
* Random Deathmatch (RDM)
* Pushing players to their death

Stealing may lead to in-game repercussions from the player that you stole from.

### 2.4 Scamming is not allowed.
Scamming other players, either for in-game or real-world benefit is not allowed.

### 2.5 Collecting unclaimed valuable blocks is allowed.
You may break and keep any unclaimed items from the following list:
* Iron
* Gold 
* Emerald 
* Diamond 
* Netherite 
* Beacon 
* Conduit 
* Brewing Stand  
* Beehives & Bee Nests 
* Banners 
* Map Art 
* Siege Engines 
* Player & Decorative Heads
* Crops
* Copper (all stages)
* Sponges
* Anvils

### 2.6 Exploiting bugs or unintended mechanics is not allowed.
Using glitches or bugs to gain an advantage or harm others will result in punishment.

### 2.7 Accessing the Nether roof is not allowed.
Even temporary or accidental access violates the rules.

### 2.8 Hacks or unauthorized clients are not allowed.
Only play using vanilla or approved modifications.

### 2.9 Builds cannot be purely utility
Any builds that focus purely on utility over aesthetics are not allowed. For example, you are not allowed to build a massive cobblestone dropper as a mob farm, but if you make an effort to make the mob farm fit into the surrounding buildings that is allowed.

### 2.10 Offensive or inappropriate builds are not allowed.
This includes phallic structures, hate symbols, or similar inappropriate content.

### 2.11 Removing ruins is allowed if the area is unused for an extended period of time.
You may clear ruins for rebuilding, if they have been abandoned for 60 days, if unsure make a support ticket.

### 2.12 Terraforming must be natural and purposeful.
Large-scale terrain edits should be cohesive and achievable. When in doubt, open a support ticket.

### 2.13 Real-world structure replicas are not allowed.
Inspired designs are fine—direct recreations (e.g., Eiffel Tower) are not.

### 2.14 Map art or pixel art is not allowed.
Art that appears on the in-game map, even if well intended is not allowed

### 2.15 You may have up to three active accounts.
The first three accounts to log in will be considered your three active accounts. Additional accounts will be banned.

## 3.0 Town & Nation Rules
These rules govern the creation, naming, spacing, and structure of towns and nations to support realism, immersion, and respectful expansion.

### 3.1 Do not build towns or outposts too close to others without permission.
Towns and outposts must be at least 4 chunks (64 blocks) away from another. If you receive permission from the nearby town, you may build closer—but once granted, that agreement is permanent and cannot be withdrawn.

### 3.2 Each town may have only two outposts.
The outpost must be located within 16 chunks of the town’s main claim area.

### 3.3 Exclaves and spaghetti claims are not allowed.
Towns must claim land cohesively. Avoid fragmented claims or using thin “land bridges” to reach distant areas.

### 3.4 Town and nation names must be appropriate.
Town names may not include offensive language, or references to real-life situations, countries, people, etc. that could cause discomfort for other players.

## 4.0 Roleplay Rules
Staff do not enforce **any** rules on roleplay, except for the following.

### 4.1 Do not roleplay discriminatory or explicit themes.
Content involving slavery, racism, homophobia, genocide, bigotry, or sexual acts is strictly forbidden in any roleplay.

### 4.2 No romantic RP between adults and minors.
An adult is anyone aged 18 or older. If you're engaging in romantic RP, it is the adult's responsibility to ensure the other party is also an adult.

## 5.0 Combat Rules
PvP is enabled on Alathra Beyond, but it is governed by specific rules to ensure fairness and respect between players. Combat  is allowed — **as long as it abides by the guidelines**. Violating these rules may result in punishment.

### 5.1 RDM (Random Deathmatch) is not allowed.
RDM refers to the unjustified or non-consensual killing of other players. It undermines roleplay and disrupts the experience for others.

**What counts as RDM**:
* Attacking someone without proper warning or reason.
* Repeatedly targeting the same player or group without cause.


**What does not count as RDM**:
* Defending yourself or retaliating after being attacked—provided it’s proportionate.
* Battles during staff-approved events where PvP is explicitly permitted.
* PvP that is clearly consensual, where all parties agree to fight.
* PvP as a consequence of stealing in-game.

### 5.2 Always warn trespassers before initiating combat.
You must ask a trespasser to leave and allow at least 60 seconds for them to respond before engaging in combat. This ensures fairness and gives players time to comply or flee. If a player has already been outlawed using the towny plugin then you do not need to issue a warning asking them to leave, they will have already been issued with one via the plug in.

### 5.3 No more than 10 animals in combat.
Total animals per person, not per animal type. All animals fall under this rule.

## 6.0 Reporting Rules
### 6.1 Directly messaging (DM) staff members to make reports is not allowed.
Reports must be submitted through the official Discord ticket system. Messaging staff privately slows down the process and can lead to confusion or missed reports.

### 6.2 Use the ticket system on Discord [(#support-ticket)](https://discord.com/channels/1479820893957001216/1479864963307798640) for all reports and punishment appeals.
If you witness a rule violation or experience an issue, open a ticket as soon as possible. Delaying reports or collecting evidence over an extended time can reduce the effectiveness of investigations and may lead to inaction.

### 6.3 Select the correct ticket type and complete the required form when using the ticket system.
When submitting a ticket, select the correct ticket category and complete the required form. This helps staff understand and respond to your issue quickly and accurately.

#### Ticket Types:
* Support Ticket — Questions about the server, plugins, or anything else.
* Management Ticket — For reporting highly serious situations that go beyond the context of the game and require discretion and serious action.
* Player Report — Used to report a player.
* Ban Appeal — Used to appeal a punishment. 

### 6.4 Serious accusations must be supported by clear and relevant evidence.
False or unsupported claims will not be tolerated and may lead to disciplinary action against the accuser. Providing screenshots, chat logs or recordings of incidents are very helpful to the staff team when making a report. Reports made in good faith with valid proof are always welcomed. 

## 7.0 Wiki Rules
The wiki serves to document the world, lore, nations, towns, and community creations that happen on the server. It is a collaborative resource built by players to preserve the history and stories of Alathra Beyond. These rules exist to ensure the wiki remains organized, respectful, and useful for everyone. 

### 7.1 Treat others with respect.
Do not make personal attacks on other people. Do not use bigoted language, including slurs that degrade another person or group of people based on gender, race, sexual orientation, nationality, religion, etc.

### 7.2 Stay on topic.
Articles should remain focused on Alathra Beyond, its towns, nations, lore, mechanics, and community history. Unrelated articles may be deleted. This includes marking your page in the correct category, if your unsure what category best suits your page make a [Support Ticket](https://discord.com/channels/1479820893957001216/1479864963307798640/1479973241337675848) on our Discord.

### 7.3 Respect player creations.
Towns, nations, religions, and characters often represent significant effort by players. Do not alter another player's page without their permission.

### 7.4 Do not engage in excessive self-promotion.
The wiki is a collaborative community resource for Alathra Beyond. It is NOT a free place to advertise your related website, YouTube channel, blog, social media account, etc. If your unsure your link would be advertising, make a [Support Ticket](https://discord.com/channels/1479820893957001216/1479864963307798640/1479973241337675848).

### 7.5 Use clear and organized writing.
Structure pages using headings, sections, and templates whenever possible. Well-formatted pages helps keep the wiki easy to read, navigate, and maintain. If you are unsure how to format a page, use the example pages that are automatically provided when creating pages from category pages.

### 7.6 Follow Fandom's Community Guidelines.
You can find the Fandom's Community Guidelines [here](https://community.fandom.com/wiki/Fandom_Community_Guidelines).

### 7.7 Follow staff decisions.
Staff may make decisions regarding page content, formatting, or disputes to maintain the health of the wiki.

## Protocols
Protocols are guidelines used by the staff team to ensure consistent and fair handling of specific situations. While not formal rules, these standardized processes help maintain transparency and order. This section outlines how the staff typically responds to certain events, so players know what to expect.

### Banned Player Inventories
Staff will **not retrieve or distribute** the inventory, items, or balance of a banned player — even at their request.

### Banned Player Towns
When a town’s owner is banned, staff will **only transfer ownership** to a current member of the town. Ownership will be reassigned using the following process:
* **First**, the next highest-ranking and longest-residing member of the town will be given ownership.
* **If no such member exists**, a 7-day window will open during which **any current town resident** may apply for ownership. The first applicant will receive ownership.
* **If no one comes** forward within 7 days, the town will be **marked as ruined**.
